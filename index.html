Traveler Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 18, 2026

Traveler ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, stored, and your rights when using Traveler.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Firebase authentication data
  • Unique user identifier (Firebase UID)

User Content

You may create and store:

  • Trips and itineraries
  • Events and schedules
  • Budgets and expenses
  • Reviews, notes, and memories
  • Place descriptions and travel details

Shared Trips

If you share a trip, invited users may view and edit its content. You are responsible for sharing data only with trusted individuals.

Location and Search Data

Traveler does not access or collect your device GPS location.

When you search for places, your text input may be sent to Geoapify to return relevant results.

Automatically Collected Data

We do not use analytics or advertising tracking tools. Limited technical data may be processed for authentication, security, and app functionality.

How We Use Information

  • Provide and operate the app
  • Store and sync travel plans
  • Enable shared trip collaboration
  • Display maps and events
  • Calculate budgets and summaries
  • Maintain security and reliability

Data Storage and Retention

Data is stored using Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore.

We retain data while your account is active. When you delete your account, all associated data including profile information, trips, events, and related content is permanently deleted, subject to reasonable processing time.

Data Deletion

You may delete your account at any time. This will remove:

  • Your profile
  • All trips and events
  • All budgets and user content

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data.

Data may be shared only:

  • With users you explicitly share trips with
  • With service providers (Firebase, Geoapify)
  • If required by law

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud Firestore
  • Geoapify

These providers have their own privacy policies governing their data use.

Tracking and Analytics

Traveler does not use analytics, advertising networks, or cross-app tracking.

Data Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your data. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Traveler is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly allow users under 18 to register or use the app.

Your Rights

  • Access and update your data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Stop using the app at any time

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of Traveler means you accept the updated policy.
